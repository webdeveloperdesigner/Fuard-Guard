Frequently Asked Questions

What is FraudGuard?

FraudGuard is an advanced fraud detection system designed to monitor transactions and identify suspicious activities in real-time.

How does the fraud detection work?

FraudGuard utilizes machine learning algorithms to analyze transaction patterns and detect anomalies, improving accuracy over time.

Can I customize alert settings?

Yes, you can customize alert settings to tailor notifications to your needs, ensuring you are alerted about the most relevant threats.

How can I access my transaction reports?

Reports can be generated from the Reports section and scheduled for periodic emails.
